Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how FoodSpin ("we", "our", or "us") handles your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

FoodSpin stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Restaurant names you add to your lists
  • Custom list names you create
  • Visit history and exclusion preferences
  • App settings and preferences

Automatically Collected Information

We may collect limited anonymous analytics data through third-party services:

  • App crashes and performance data (via Apple)
  • General usage statistics (no personal data)
  • Device type and iOS version for compatibility

How We Use Your Information

The information stored in FoodSpin is used solely to:

  • Provide the restaurant selection functionality
  • Save your preferences and settings
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Provide customer support when requested

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: All your restaurant data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your restaurant lists or personal preferences.

No Cloud Sync: FoodSpin does not upload your data to any servers. Your information remains private on your device.

Backup: When you use the backup feature, data is exported to your chosen location (iCloud Drive, Files app, etc.) under your control.

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

Free version users will see advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising identifier
  • IP address (for location targeting)
  • General usage data

Premium users do not see any advertisements and are not subject to ad-related data collection.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Your Rights and Choices

  • Data Deletion: You can delete all app data by uninstalling FoodSpin
  • Opt-out of Ads: Upgrade to Premium to remove all advertisements
  • Limit Ad Tracking: Use iOS Settings → Privacy → Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking

Children's Privacy

FoodSpin is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
