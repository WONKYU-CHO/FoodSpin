Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create multiple restaurant lists?

Upgrade to FoodSpin Premium to create unlimited lists for different occasions. Free users can manage one list with all their favorite restaurants.

Can I avoid recently visited restaurants?

Yes! In Settings, you can enable "Exclude recent visits" and choose how many days to exclude (1-30 days). This prevents the wheel from selecting places you've just been to.

How do I restore my Premium purchase?

Go to Settings → Premium → Restore Purchase. This will restore your Premium access if you've purchased it before.

Is FoodSpin available in my language?

FoodSpin supports 27 languages! Go to Settings → Language to change your preferred language.

How do I backup my restaurant lists?

Premium users can backup their data through Settings → Backup & Restore. Export your lists to save them or import to restore.

Does FoodSpin work offline?

Yes! Once installed, FoodSpin works completely offline. All your restaurant data is stored locally on your device.

Troubleshooting

App crashes or freezes

  • Make sure you're running the latest version of FoodSpin
  • Try restarting your device
  • Check if your iOS is up to date

Premium purchase not working

  • Ensure you're signed in with the correct Apple ID
  • Try the "Restore Purchase" option in Settings
  • Check your internet connection
