Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add restaurants to my list?

Tap the "+" button in the app to add a new restaurant. You can add as many restaurants as you like and organize them into custom lists.

Can I backup my restaurant lists?

Yes! FoodSpin allows you to export your data to iCloud Drive or the Files app. Go to Settings in the app to find the backup options.

How do I remove ads?

You can upgrade to FoodSpin Premium to enjoy an ad-free experience. The upgrade option is available in the app's settings.

Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely. All your restaurant data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect or store any of your personal information on our servers.

What iOS version do I need?

FoodSpin requires iOS 17.0 or later and is compatible with iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch.
